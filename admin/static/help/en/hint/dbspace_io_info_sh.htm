DBSpace I/O Information

This page shows size and disk I/O status of each dbspace managed by the monitored database server*. Information exhibited on this page includes dbspace name, dbspace type, the number of chunks assigned to a dbspace, size of allocated and used data storage, and the amount and times of disk I/O occurred on a dbspace. According to the I/O information of a dbspace, you may need to drill down to check information of chunks and/or tables in the dbspace, and to check read/write and utilization of the database for diagonising and analyzing disk I/O problem.

Related drill-down options in the right-click menu : Object-Level Trend - DBSpace Page Reads/Writes, DBSpace Overview Panel, Chunk I/O Information, Chunk Information, Top Busy Table, Table Extent List.

You can click a column head to sort the list in ascending/descending order of the selected column and click or anytime to refresh information on the page.
