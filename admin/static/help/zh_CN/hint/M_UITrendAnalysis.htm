Trend Analysis

dbSonar offers the unique feature of displaying trend charts in real-time or over a specific period of time for various RDBMS performance indicators. The historical data saved in dbSonar's repository allow you to see trend charts of multiple KPIs simultaneously and help you to drill down to the root of the problem, irrespective of when it occurred. Moreover, trend charts enable you to see instantaneous values of the selected KPIs by clicking anywhere on the trend chart as well as view SQL at the point of time selected on the trend chart.

  • Trend Selection :  It enables you to select the KPIs whose trend charts you would like to view.

  • Sampling Rate :  Select an sampling rate from the Time Mode drop-down box to take samples of the selected KPI. That is, samples taken at this sampling rate are displayed on the trend chart. For instance, if you specify the Hourly option, samples taken at every hour-interval are displayed on the trend chart. You may choose a sampling rate from 5 Minutes, Hourly, or Daily.

  • Datetime Range :  The default date and time range of the trend chart is from now up to 3 days ago for historical performance analysis. You may specify a custom start and end points of time interval by clicking icon to bring up a calendar pop-up window.

  • Click Redraw to redraw trend charts manually.

  • Click Clean all chart to clean up all displayed trend charts, legend-value pairs and selections in Trend List to start a new graphical analysis.

  • Trend Chart :  It displays trend charts of the selected KPIs. Use any of the following steps to view additional options for the trend charts:

    • Legend-Value table :  Click to select any instant of time on trend charts. A red line shows at the selected instant of time and the instantaneous values for the selected instant of time are shown under this table.

    • Right-click to select any instant of time on trend charts and drill down to view SQL executed at the point of time
