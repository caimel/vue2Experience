DBSpace Information

Monitoring usage of all dbspaces managed in the monitored database server* in order to determine if extra chunk is required for a DBSpace. Information exhibited on the page includes the dbspace type, the number of chunks assigned to a dbspace, total size, free size, usage percentage in bar chart and the threshold for sending an alert. You may get more utilization information of chunks and/or tables on a dbspace through drill-down options.

Related drill-down options in the right-click menu : Chunk I/O Information, Chunk Information, Top Busy Table, Table Extent List.

You can click a column head to sort the list in ascending/descending order of the selected column and click or anytime to refresh information on the page.
