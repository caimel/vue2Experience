Content Area

This is an information displaying area. It can contain more than one tab and will always have the MainPanel tab shown. When you select an item from the SideBar or drill down from a selected item in the Content Area, a new tab will be launched in the Content Area to display information of the selected item. Information exhibited in a tab can be a tabular list, a trend chart(s) and/or a bar chart etc. You can click more >> on the specific tab to get more information about what the tab is, what information and/or functionality is provided in the tab.

You may click on a tab to close the tab itself, or right click a tab head and select an option from the popup menu as shown below to close the tab itself, all tabs except the MainPanel tab or all other tabs except the MainPanel tab and the one where the popup menu comes from.

For a tabular information, you can click a column head to sort the list in ascending/descending order of the selected column.

For a bar chart, you can change the y-axis of the chart by Chart Option option in the right-click menu of the chart.

For a trend chart, you will see a red line displayed on the trend chart as you click any place on the chart and the point-in-time value under the red line is displayed in the Legend-Value table.
